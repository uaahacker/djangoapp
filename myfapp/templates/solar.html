Solar Energy

Solar energy is the conversion of sunlight into electricity, either directly using photovoltaics (PV), or indirectly using concentrated solar power (CSP). Concentrated solar power systems use lenses or mirrors and tracking systems to focus a large area of sunlight into a small beam. Photovoltaic cells convert light into an electric current using the photovoltaic effect.

Solar energy is a clean and renewable source of energy that has a minimal impact on the environment. It can be used for various applications, including generating electricity, heating water for residential or commercial use, and even powering vehicles.

Advancements in solar technology have led to increased efficiency and affordability, making solar energy a viable and increasingly popular option for sustainable energy production.
